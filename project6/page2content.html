Recommendations

Madrid

I could go on for hours about the amazing restaurants in Madrid. But just a few that I highly recommend going to are Salvaje, Platea, Mercado de San Miguel and Takos al Pastor.

Valencia

Valencia had the best paella and a place I would reccomend going for it would be Casa Eulogio.

Italy

Ristorante Marco Polo is an amazing resturaunt in Venice, Italy. If you are visiting Florence, I recommend going to Trattoria Za Za. Not only do they have such good pasta options but also at a very affordable price.

Switzerland

If you visit Interlaken, you must go to funky chocolate club. This place is adorable and has the best chocolate ever. If you visit Geneva, sprungli is the best. I even had to bring some back home to the United States!

Amsterdam

While in Amsterdam, you must go to Pancakes Amsterdam. It is right by the Anne Frank House and is a great spot to visit. The pancakes were so good and light too!
